F.A.Q

  • Aluminum Composite Panel(ACP)

    ACP is a three-layer sandwich panel consisting of two pre-painted aluminum sheets bonded to a Low-density polyethylene(LDPE) core. Available in 3 to 6mm thickness

  • ACP Sheet Colors

    ACP sheet panels offers a wide selection of more than 300+ colors and textures like wood, stone, mirror, 3D etc...

  • ACP Sheet Applications

    Modular Kitchen | False Ceilings | Cupboards | Wardrobe | Hospitals | Schools | Offices | Hotels | Partition & Cabinets | Shopping Malls | Multiplexes | Signage | Gates & Louvers & Pergolas | Facade or Wall paneling | Column & Beams Wraps | Showroom Finishes

  • ACP Sheet Benefits & Features

    Termite and Borer resistant | Strong material | Customizable | Lightweight | Durable | Flexible | Rigidity | Low weight on building | Modern and Classy | Easy to maintain | Anti-corrosion | Anti-chalking | Anti-fungal | Easily bent, fold, turn | Chemical Resistant | Weather-resistant | Stain-resistant | Fire-retardant | UV resistant | Wide colors & textures | Eco & Budget-friendly | Easily recyclable | Easy installation | Unbreakable

  • Why Choose ACP Sheet

    No other material is flexible as the ACP Sheets, Moreover; it is a modern and lots of practical & technical advantages over traditional materials.
